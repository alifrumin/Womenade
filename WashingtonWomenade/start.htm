How to Start Your Own Womenade

Organize a group of friends: These are the co-founders of your local chapter of Womenade!

Find a worthy cause in your community.

Decide what kind of party you want to throw - potluck, picnic, barbeque - you name it!

Strategize and plan. Decide what your party will need and how you will take care of your funds. Ask local businesses to help out!

Send out invitations, with a letter of explanation telling your guests all about the Womenade idea, and about the local cause they will be donating to.

Have a party!!! Keep track of your guests' contributions and addresses, so you can create a mailing list and keep your finances organized.

Eat, Drink, Be Merry....and Do Good. When you have Women, Make Womenade.
