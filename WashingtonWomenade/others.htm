Womenade across America

To date, at least 38 local Womenade chapters have sprouted up across the USA. If you type in Womenade to Google, you will find articles about many of them.   Some chapters were started when local women read the article about Washington Womenade in Real Simple Magazine, others when local newspapers reprinted a Washington Post article about our potlucks. These local Womenades fund a wide variety of causes, from homelessness, to pediatric cancer, to ambulances, to helping local immigrant families who have recently arrived in various communities to get on their feet.  

We can't vouch for ongoing parties as of this date, but we know that at least once in the last few years there have been Womenade events in the following towns and cities of the USA:
